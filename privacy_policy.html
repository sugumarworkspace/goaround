Information Collection and Use

Goaround does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive information from users. No data is transmitted to external servers or third-party services. All gameplay data remains within the device.

Goaround is designed to be safe and enjoyable for children. The app does not collect personal information from children or adults. It does not include ads, social media links, or online interactions. Parents can allow children to use the app without concern about privacy risks.
